One Person Company

CRR Services for One Person Company Registration

One person company is hybrid of Sole-Proprietorship and Private Limited Company, where it is easy to incorporate and mange with less compliance compared to other forms of corporate registration (i.e. Private Limited, Public Limited). In OPC the all the controlling and powers rest with the single person (however, a director nominee is present, but has zero power until the real director proves incapable of carrying on).
